# Privacy Policy for KV Player

**Last Updated: July 2026**

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how **KV Player** ("the Application") handles information when you use the software. 

Because KV Player is a local, offline media player, our privacy policy is very simple: **We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information.**

---

## 1. Information Collection and Use
KV Player does not collect any personal data, usage telemetry, or device identifiers. 
* **No Telemetry:** We do not track your playback habits, what files you open, or how long you use the application.
* **No Analytics:** There are no analytics SDKs or third-party tracking scripts integrated into this software.
* **No Account Required:** You do not need to register an account or provide any personal details (such as your name, email, or phone number) to use the application.

## 2. Local File Access
KV Player requires access to your local storage to open and play the media files (video and audio) that you explicitly select.
* **Local Processing only:** All video and audio decoding, scaling, and rendering are performed entirely on your local CPU and GPU.
* **No Transmission:** The file paths and contents of the media you play never leave your computer. We do not upload or stream your local files to any external servers.

## 3. Internet Connectivity
KV Player does not require an internet connection to function. It operates completely offline. The application does not establish any network connections, transmit data packets over the internet, or communicate with external servers.

## 4. Third-Party Services and Advertisements
KV Player is completely ad-free. We do not integrate with third-party advertising networks, nor do we share any data with third parties.

## 5. Children's Privacy
Because we do not collect any personal information, KV Player is safe for use by individuals of all ages, including children under the age of 13.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates.
